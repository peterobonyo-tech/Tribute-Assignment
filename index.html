TRIBUTE TO RAILA AMOLLO ODINGA

The Father of democracy

Raila was a brother, friend, and patriot whose life was dedicated to the service of Kenya. Raila’s passing left my heart heavy, we shared struggles, moments of unity, and vision for a better Kenya would remain etched in history. I loved Raila’s resilience, courage in the face of adversity, and unwavering commitment to democracy, justice, and the rights of all Kenyans.

NOTABLE ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Most effective opposition leader in Kenya history
  • Prime Minister of Kenya
  • Roads Minister in NARC Government
